ESP32-based Platform Game

ongoing

Inspired from the universe of The Lord of the Rings, this platform game currently has 2 worlds the player can explore: the Shire and the Mines of Moria. 10 minutes of average game time can be expected for the first runs, accounting for a few deaths leading the player to start over. Although, with persistence, the 'fake ring' may be retrieved from the depth of the Moria...

Hardware-wise, the program is running on an ESP32 (the "console") and displays on an external 128x160 LCD screen at a steady 45 frames per second, with a 16-bit color depth. The player dynamically interacts with its character using a wireless gamepad (joystick + buttons) communicating with the console via Bluetooth LE. The game also allows music and various sounds to be played via a buzzer, the way some consoles of the 70's used to.

This project uses no external library (only the ESP-IDF API's) and has its own peripheral drivers and game engine. Click here to know more about the technical details!

5-Axis Robotic Arm Build & Firmware

completed

Started during the plentiful free-time period that was the COVID-19 2020 lockdown, this project is at the boundaries between my love for mechanical engineering and programming. The whole purpose behind the design and building of this robot was to get a first foot in the world of robotics, and learn the intriguing ways of controlling servo motors. Plus, having a desktop-sized robotic arm at your side can always make a good friend.

The robot, able to carry up to 1kg of payload, is made of 6 servo motors (cheap MG995 & SG90), 2 transmissions and no less than 44 PLA 3D-printed parts.

As one of my first embedded project, I based the 5-axis arm on the Teensy 4.0 board, using the Arduino framework, giving me a straightforward start. The firmware allows the user to control the effector of the robot using a cartesian coordinate system. The inverse kinematics algorithm within the firmware takes care of feeding the motors with the proper angular positions, as well as managing the dynamics of the system.
